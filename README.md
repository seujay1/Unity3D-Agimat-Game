### **Level 2: Abandoned Village**
- **Setting:** After escaping the forest, the hiker finds a deserted village with flickering lights and an eerie atmosphere.  
- **Backstory:** The villagers who tried to steal Agimats mysteriously disappeared. Spirits and enemies now haunt the area.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Collect tokens or letters to find the exit.  
  - Avoid enemies that spawn randomly.  
  - Place Agimats in their rightful locations to unlock the next level.  
- **Mechanics:**  
  - Enemies are banished if shone with a flashlight for 5 seconds.  
  - Enemies continually respawn to maintain challenge.

### **Level 3: The Cave**
- **Setting:** Hidden cave behind the forest. Dimly lit with glowing crystals and dripping water.  
- **Backstory:** The cave is a sacred passage disturbed by villagers seeking Agimat power. The Tiktik now guards it.  
- **Objective:**  
  - Collect 3 runestones scattered in the cave.  
  - Avoid or hide from patrolling spirits.  
  - Place collected items on the altar to unlock deeper levels.  
- **Gameplay:**  
  - Flashlight drains faster or flickers.  
  - Checkpoints activate after placing items on the altar.
